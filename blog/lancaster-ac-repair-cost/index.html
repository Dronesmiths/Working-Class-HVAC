How Much Does AC Repair Cost in Lancaster?

Transparency is key. Here is what you can expect to pay for common HVAC repairs.

Posted on July 10, 2025 | Location: Lancaster, CA

One of the biggest anxieties homeowners have when their AC breaks is: "How much is this going to cost?" At Working Class HVAC, we believe in transparent, flat-rate pricing. We aren't here to upsell you.

The Service Call Fee

Most HVAC companies in Lancaster charge a dispatch or diagnostic fee to get a truck to your house. In the Antelope Valley, this typically ranges from $89 to $129. This covers the technician's time, gas, and expertise to find the problem.

Common Repair Costs

  • Capacitors: This is the most common failure in our heat. A capacitor start kit or replacement typically costs between $150 - $350 depending on the unit size.
  • Contactor Relays: The switch that turns your AC on. Replacement usually runs $200 - $400.
  • Fan Motors: If the wind killed your fan motor, you are likely looking at $500 - $900 for parts and labor.
  • Refrigerant Charge: Freon (R-410a) prices fluctuate, but a recharge can cost anywhere from $100 to $200 per pound.

Repair vs. Replace?

If your repair quote is over $1,500 and your system is more than 15 years old, it might be time to stop throwing good money after bad. Check our guide on 5 signs your AC is failing.

Want a specific quote? We can usually give you a ballpark over the phone if you describe the symptoms.
