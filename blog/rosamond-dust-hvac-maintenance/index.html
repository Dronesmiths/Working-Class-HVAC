The Rosamond Dust Battle: Protecting Your AC

Living in the Kern County desert means dust is a way of life. Don't let it kill your system.

Posted on August 5, 2025 | Location: Rosamond, CA

If you live in Rosamond, you know the drill: You dust your furniture, and two hours later, it looks like you never touched it. That same fine alkali dust is being sucked into your air conditioner every time it turns on.

The 30-Day Rule

In most cities, you can change your filter every 90 days. In Rosamond, we recommend checking it every 30 days, especially during windy seasons. A clogged filter is the #1 cause of compressor failure in our area.

Coil Impact

Dust that bypasses your filter lands on the indoor evaporator coil. When this mixes with the condensation (water) the coil produces, it turns into mud. This mud acts like an insulator, preventing the coil from absorbing heat. The result? Your electric bill shoots up, and your house stays warm.
