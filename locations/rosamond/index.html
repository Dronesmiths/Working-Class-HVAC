Rosamond HVAC FAQs

Do you charge extra for Rosamond?

No. Even though it's across the county line, we consider Rosamond part of our local service area. You pay the exact same rates as our Lancaster customers.

My filters get dirty really fast here. Why?

Southern Kern County is notoriously dusty due to the open desert. We recommend checking your filter every 30 days during peak windy seasons to protect your system.

Can you replace my mobile home furnace?

Yes. Rosamond has many manufactured homes, and they require specific "HUD-approved" furnaces. We are certified to install and repair these specialized units.
