Tehachapi HVAC FAQs

Do you travel up to Tehachapi for service calls?

Yes! We regularly service Tehachapi, Golden Hills, Bear Valley Springs, and Stallion Springs.

My heat pump is freezing up in winter, is that normal?

Tehachapi winters can be harsh. Older heat pumps struggle below freezing. We can check your defrost cycle or recommend a dual-fuel system for better efficiency.

Can you install AC in a home that only has heating?

Yes. Many older Tehachapi homes lack AC. We can install ductless mini-splits or central air systems to add cooling without major renovations.
