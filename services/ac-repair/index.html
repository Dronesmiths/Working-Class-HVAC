Common AC Questions

Why is my AC freezing up?

Ice on the coils usually restricts airflow. This is caused by a dirty filter or low refrigerant levels. Turn the unit off immediately to prevent compressor damage and call us.

How often should I service my AC?

We recommend a tune-up once a year, preferably in the spring before the summer heat hits. This helps prevent unexpected breakdowns and extends system life.

What is a SEER rating?

SEER stands for Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio. Higher SEER numbers mean the unit is more efficient and costs less to operate. California requires a minimum of SEER2 14.3.
