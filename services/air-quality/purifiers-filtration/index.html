Maximum Filtration for Desert Living

The High Desert presents unique challenges for indoor air quality. Between high winds and seasonal pollen, your home can quickly become a trap for allergens. Working Class HVAC provides advanced filtration solutions that capture microscopic particles before they reach your family.

Advanced Filtration Technologies:

  • HEPA Filtration: Captures 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 microns.
  • Media Air Cleaners: High-capacity filters that last up to a year and catch fine dust.
  • Electronic Air Purifiers: Uses electrical charges to pull contaminants out of the air stream.
  • PCO (Photo-Catalytic Oxidation): Destroys viruses and bacteria at the molecular level.

Health Benefits

  • Significant reduction in allergy and asthma triggers.
  • Elimination of pet dander and smoke odors.
  • Cleaner ductwork and increased HVAC system life.
