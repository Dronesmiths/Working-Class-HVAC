Breathe Smarter with Modern Ventilation

Modern homes are built to be airtight for energy efficiency. While this saves on utility bills, it can lead to "Sick Building Syndrome"—where pollutants like VOCs, pet odors, and CO2 become trapped inside. Working Class HVAC installs ventilation systems that exchange stale indoor air for fresh outdoor air without losing your heating or cooling.

Ventilation Technologies:

  • ERV (Energy Recovery Ventilators): Ideal for Lancaster's climate, ERVs transfer both heat and moisture to keep your home comfortable.
  • HRV (Heat Recovery Ventilators): Efficiently transfers heat from outgoing air to incoming fresh air.
  • Mechanical Ventilation: Controlled exhaust systems for kitchens, baths, and high-smoke areas.
  • Whole-House Fans: A High Desert staple for massive evening cooling during summer.

The Fresh Air Advantage

Proper ventilation isn't just about comfort—it's a critical safety requirement for modern residences.

  • Reduction of VOCs and airborne Chemicals.
  • Lower CO2 levels for improved mental clarity.
  • Controlled moisture levels to prevent mold.
