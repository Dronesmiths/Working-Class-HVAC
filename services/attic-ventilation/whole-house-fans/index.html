Natural Cooling for Your Entire Home

In the High Desert, summer nights often drop to a comfortable 65°F while your house remains trapped at 85°F. Whole house fans utilize this cool outside air, pulling it through your windows and exhausting the hot, stale air out through your attic vents. At Working Class HVAC, we specialize in QuietCool installation—the gold standard in whisper-quiet, ultra-efficient home ventilation.

Why Choose a Whole House Fan?

  • Massive Energy Savings: Running a whole house fan costs pennies per hour compared to your AC.
  • Rapid Air Exchange: Replace all the air in your home in just 3-4 minutes.
  • Attic Cooling: Lowers your attic temperature by up to 50 degrees, protecting your roof and insulation.
  • Improved Air Quality: Flushes out VOCs, pet dander, and cooking odors instantly.
