In the High Desert, wind and dust are constant. Over time, that fine desert silt finds its way into your air ducts, settling and recirculating every time your system kicks on. Professional air duct cleaning is essential for maintaining healthy indoor air quality and ensuring your HVAC system runs efficiently.

Our Advanced Cleaning Process:

  • HEPA-Filtered Suction: Industrial-strength vacuums ensure dust is pulled *out* of your home, not blown around it.
  • Mechanical Agitation: High-speed brushes and "whips" loosen stubborn debris from duct walls.
  • Register & Boot Sanitization: Cleaning every point of entry for your home's air.
  • Video Verification: See the difference for yourself with our internal duct cameras.

Is it time for a cleaning?

Look for these common signs of dirty ducts:

  • Visible dust blowing out of vents.
  • Musty or "stuffy" odors when the fan runs.
  • Unexplained increases in allergy or sinus symptoms.
