The Hidden Key to Efficiency

Your AC and furnace are only as good as the ductwork connected to them. In many High Desert homes, old, leaky, or crushed ducts are responsible for up to 30% of energy loss. This means you are paying to cool your attic, not your living room.

Ductwork FAQs

How much does duct replacement cost?

It depends on the size of your home and the accessibility of your attic. We provide free estimates to give you an exact price.

Can you clean my old ducts?

We can clean them if they are structurally sound. However, if the inner liner is torn or brittle, replacement is the better option to prevent fiberglass from entering your air.

What is R-8 insulation?

R-8 is the current California standard for duct insulation value. It resists heat transfer much better than the older R-4 or R-6 ducts found in many AV homes.
