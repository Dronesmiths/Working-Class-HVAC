Why Duct Insulation Matters in the AV

In Lancaster and Palmdale, attic temperatures can easily exceed 140°F in the summer and drop below freezing in the winter. If your ducts aren't properly insulated, your air loses its energy before it ever reaches your rooms. Working Class HVAC upgrades old, thin insulation to modern R-8 standards, ensuring your comfort isn't lost in the attic.

Our Insulation Services:

  • R-8 Upgrade: California's current standard for maximum thermal resistance.
  • Vapor Barrier Audit: Ensuring your insulation is protected from moisture and condensation.
  • Crumbling insulation Removal: Safe removal of old, degraded fiberglass or rockwool.
  • Duct Wrap: Applying thick, reflective barriers to metal ducting for added protection.

Stay Protected

Old insulation (R-4 or R-6) is simply not enough for the extreme temperature swings of the Antelope Valley.

  • Cold air stays colder on the way to your vents.
  • Reduction in "Standby Loss" when the system is off.
  • Eliminates condensation issues that lead to mold.
