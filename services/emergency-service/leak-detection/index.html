Stopping High-Desert Efficiency Loss

Many technicians simply "top off" a system with refrigerant and leave. At Working Class HVAC, we consider that malpractice. A leak in your AC coils or copper lines allows moisture and non-condensables to enter your system, leading to acid buildup and compressor burnout. We use nitrogen pressure testing and electronic sniffer technology to locate microscopic leaks in evaporator coils and flare fittings, ensuring your repair is permanent.

Our Leak Detection Process:

  • Electronic Sniffing: sensitive detection of R-410A and R-22 molecules.
  • Ultrasonic Detection: "hearing" the high-frequency whistle of a leak.
  • Nitrogen Pressure Testing: isolating system sections to find the exact failure point.
  • Precision Brazing: oxygen-free soldering to repair line sets permanently.
  • Evacuation & Micron Pull: ensuring a deep vacuum before recharging.

The Purity Edge
