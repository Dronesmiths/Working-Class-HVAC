Natural Cooling for a Desert Climate

Evaporative coolers (commonly known as swamp coolers) are perfectly suited for the Lancaster and Palmdale climate. By using the natural process of evaporation, these systems can lower indoor temperatures by 15-20 degrees while using a fraction of the electricity of a standard AC unit. They also add much-needed moisture to our dry desert air.

Our Evaporative Cooler Services:

  • Standard Unit Installation: High-efficiency side-draft and down-draft models.
  • Ductless Installations: Window-mounted or roof-mounted units for targeted cooling.
  • MasterCool™ Specialized Service: Certified installation of high-performance media coolers.
  • Thermostat Integration: Modern controls to automate your evaporative cooling.

The Swamp Cooler Advantage

In the High Desert, a well-maintained swamp cooler can handle about 80% of the cooling season.

  • Up to 75% lower electricity bills than central AC.
  • Adds healthy moisture to dry indoor air.
  • Environmentally friendly—no chemical refrigerants.
