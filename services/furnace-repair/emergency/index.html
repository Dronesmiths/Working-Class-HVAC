Fast Heat Restoration

When your furnace stops working in the middle of a winter storm, you can't wait until Monday morning. Working Class HVAC keeps technicians on standby to handle urgent heating failures across Lancaster, Palmdale, and Rosamond.

We Handle All Heating Emergencies:

  • Complete System Failure: Furnace won't turn on or won't blow air.
  • Gas Odors: Immediate response for suspected gas leaks (Safety First!).
  • Electrical Burning Smells: Preventing potential fire hazards in your heating unit.
  • Loud Screeching or Banging: Indicators of imminent blower motor or part failure.
  • Frequent Short-Cycling: System turning on/off every few minutes.

Rapid Dispatch

Our goal is to get your heat back on within hours, not days. We arrive in fully-stocked trucks ready to perform most repairs on the spot.

Safety Tip:

If you smell gas or suspect a carbon monoxide leak, turn off the system immediately, open windows, and evacuate the home before calling for help.
