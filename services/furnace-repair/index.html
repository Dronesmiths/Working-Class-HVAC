Common Heating Questions

Why is my heater blowing cold air?

It could be a dirty flame sensor, a bad igniter, or the pilot light is out. Sometimes it's simply a thermostat setting or a dirty filter.

How long does a furnace last?

A well-maintained gas furnace typically lasts 15-20 years. If yours is older and needs frequent repairs, it may be time to replace it.

Is Carbon Monoxide a risk?

Yes. Any gas burning appliance produces CO. A cracked heat exchanger can leak this odorless gas into your home. We highly recommend annual inspections and CO detectors.
