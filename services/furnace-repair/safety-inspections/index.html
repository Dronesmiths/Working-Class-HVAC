Why Safety Inspections Matter

A gas furnace is a powerful appliance that relies on controlled combustion. If a component like the heat exchanger becomes cracked or the flue becomes blocked, it can leak odorless, deadly carbon monoxide into your home. Our safety inspections are designed to catch these issues before they become emergencies.

Our 12-Point Heating Safety Protocol:

  • Carbon Monoxide Testing: Electronic detection around the unit and vents.
  • Heat Exchanger Inspection: Checking for cracks, rust, or weak spots.
  • Flame Sensor Cleaning: Ensuring reliable ignition and preventing "lockouts."
  • Gas Line Leak Check: Verifying the integrity of all heating fuel lines.
  • Flue & Venting Audit: Ensuring toxic exhaust is exiting your home correctly.
  • Safety Switch Validation: Testing the limits that shut down the unit if it overheats.

Protect Your Family

Annual inspections are required by most major manufacturers to maintain your system's warranty.
