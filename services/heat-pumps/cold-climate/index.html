Mastering the Winter Heat Pump Challenge

While heat pumps are incredibly efficient, they face their toughest test when Antelope Valley temperatures drop below 40°F. Without proper calibration and auxiliary heat sequencing, their performance can suffer. Working Class HVAC specializes in "Cold Climate Performance" packages that tune your system's defrost logic, verify refrigerant levels for heating mode, and ensure your backup heat strips engage only when absolutely necessary.

Our Winter performance Audit:

  • Defrost Logic verification: ensuring the outdoor coil doesn't turn into a block of ice.
  • Reversing Valve Testing: precise verification of the heating/cooling switch mechanism.
  • Auxiliary Heat Calibration: Adjusting outdoor thermostats to prevent expensive electric heat overuse.
  • Heating mode Refrigerant Check: specialized monitoring of high-side pressures for winter demand.
  • Crankcase Heater Verification: Protecting the compressor from liquid slugging on cold starts.

Winter Security

  • Lower winter utility bills through heat strip management.
  • Constant, reliable warmth even during sub-freezing nights.
  • Extended equipment life by reducing compressor strain.
