Maximum Winter Heating Reliability

Before the first frost hits the High Desert, your heating system needs to be forensicly inspected for safety and performance. Carbon monoxide leaks, cracked heat exchangers, and clogged burners are invisible threats that a professional fall tune-up can detect. Working Class HVAC provides comprehensive heating service that cleans, calibrates, and verifies every critical safety component of your furnace or heat pump.

Our Fall Heating Checklist:

  • Heat Exchanger Audit: checking for structural cracks using digital analyzers.
  • Burner Cleaning: ensuring a clean, blue blue-flame combustion for maximum efficiency.
  • Limit Switch Test: verifying the safety sensors that prevent overheating.
  • Blower Motor Service: Cleaning and testing the motor that moves your air.
  • Gas Pressure verify: adjusting valves to factory-load specifications.

Fall Priority

  • Full verification of carbon monoxide safety.
  • Reduction in winter emergency breakdown repair costs.
  • Maintaining manufacturer warranty requirements.
