Forensic Cooling Readiness

The transitions from winter to high desert summer are brutal on mechanical systems. Dust, debris, and dormant electrical components need to be verified before you demand full power from your AC. Working Class HVAC provides comprehensive spring tune-ups that go beyond a simple filter change. We scrub coils, tighten electrical terminals, and calibrate thermostat response to ensure you have cold air exactly when you need it.

Our 21-Point Spring Checklist:

  • Condenser Coil cleaning: Maximizing heat transfer for lower electricity bills.
  • Refrigerant charge verify: ensuring thermodynamic efficiency is at factory specs.
  • Amp Draw Audit: testing motors and compressors to prevent sudden failures.
  • Condensate Drain flush: Preventing water damage from summer humidity clogs.
  • Contactor & Capacitor Test: verifying the electrical "muscles" of your system.

Spring Priority

  • Lower operational energy costs all summer long.
  • Reduction in 24/7 emergency repair risks.
  • Verification of factory warranty compliance.
