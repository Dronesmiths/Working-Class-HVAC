Keep Your System Healthy

Ductless units require a different type of maintenance than central HVAC. Because the indoor units process air directly in your rooms, the blower wheels and coils can become coated in dust and mold over time. Our deep cleaning process restores airflow and ensures the air you breathe is healthy.

Our Mini-Split Service Checklist:

  • Coil Deep Clean: Using specialized bibs to wash indoor coils without any mess.
  • Blower Wheel Sanitization: Removing dust and biofilm from the spinning fan.
  • Drain Line Flush: Preventing messy water leaks by clearing condensate lines.
  • Refrigerant Check: Verifying charge levels for maximum cooling power.
  • Filter Service: Industrial cleaning or replacement of multi-stage filters.

Avoid Common Issues

Regular maintenance prevents the three most common mini-split failures:

  • Water Leaks: Caused by clogged primary drains.
  • Musty Smells: Caused by bacterial growth on the blower wheel.
  • High Energy Bills: Caused by dirty coils restricting heat transfer.
