Why "Regular" Units Don't Work

Standard residential AC units are designed for large, open ductwork. Mobile home ducts are smaller and tighter. Installing a standard unit on a mobile home will often cause the coil to freeze, the compressor to fail, and your energy bills to skyrocket. We only install HUD-approved equipment design specifically for your home.
