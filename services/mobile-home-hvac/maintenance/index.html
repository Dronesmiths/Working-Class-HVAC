Specialized Care for Manufactured Homes

Mobile home HVAC systems (package units) operate under different airflow and pressure standards than standard houses. Ignoring maintenance can lead to cracked heat exchangers or blower failures due to the smaller ductwork used in manufactured homes. Our specialized mobile home tune-up ensures your system meets all safety and performance standards for the Antelope Valley climate.

Our 15-Point Inspection Highlights:

  • Duct Connection Audit: Checking the critical flex-duct seals unique to mobile homes.
  • Blower Motor Verification: Ensuring high-static performance for small registers.
  • Safety Control Testing: Verifying limit switches and HUD-required safety sensors.
  • Refrigerant Level Check: Maximizing cooling power for the desert heat.
  • Thermostat Accuracy: Ensuring your digital controls are perfectly calibrated.
