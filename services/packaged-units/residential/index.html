Compact Power for Your Home

Residential packaged units combine your furnace and AC into a single cabinet, usually located on the roof or a ground-level slab. While they save space, they require specialized maintenance to ensure the "all-in-one" design doesn't lead to complex failures. At Working Class HVAC, we have the specialized training and equipment to repair and replace residential package units from all major manufacturers.

Our Residential Package Services:

  • High-Efficiency Replacement: Upgrading to modern SEER2 units to slash utility bills.
  • Ductwork Integration: Ensuring your existing ducts can handle the airflow of a new unit.
  • Seal & Insulation Check: Maximizing energy savings by stopping leaks where the unit meets the roof.
  • Multi-Point Maintenance: Keeping your all-in-one system running peak year-round.
