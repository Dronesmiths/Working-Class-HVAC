Expert Pool Heating Restoration

Pool heat pumps are complex refrigeration systems that require a specialized HVAC technician, not just a standard pool guy. From compressor failures and TXV adjustments to refrigerant leak detection, Working Class HVAC has the technical expertise to repair your pool heating system correctly. We understand the specific demands that high desert mineral-rich water and extreme temperatures place on these units.

We diagnose & Repair:

  • Refrigerant Leaks: technical detection and EPA-certified recharging.
  • Flow Sensor Failure: Fixing "no flow" errors causing heating lockouts.
  • Compressor & Fan Repair: Restoring the high-performance heart of your unit.
  • Logic Board Failures: specialized troubleshooting of digital controls and displays.
  • Heat Exchanger scale: Cleaning and descaling to restore efficient heat transfer.
