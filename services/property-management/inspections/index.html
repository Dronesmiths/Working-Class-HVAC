Verified System Health for Peace of Mind

The turnover period is the only time you have full access to a unit without tenant scheduling conflicts. It is the critical window to perform an HVAC deep-dive. Working Class HVAC provides property managers with a comprehensive turnover audit including carbon monoxide testing, heat exchanger inspection, and static pressure checks. This documentation protects you from liability and ensures your next tenant starts with a healthy, efficient climate system.

Turnover Checklist Includes:

  • Safety verification: heat exchanger integrity and gas line leak checks.
  • Air quality Audit: inspecting evaporator coils for organic growth/neglect.
  • Performance Benchmark: verifying delta-T and airflow across all vents.
  • Filter Sanitization: standardized replacement for clean-start move-ins.
  • Digital reporting: photo-documented findings for owner billing.

The Audit Edge

  • Reduction in "first-month" tenant HVAC complaints.
  • Clear evidence of equipment condition for security deposits.
  • Extended equipment lifespan through early intervention.
