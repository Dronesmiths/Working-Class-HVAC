Engineered Airflow Distribution

Standard HVAC systems are "one-size-fits-all," but every home has unique thermal characteristics. Upstairs is always hotter, and north-facing rooms stay cold. Digital zoning solves this by installing motorized dampers in your ductwork and a master zone controller. Working Class HVAC specializes in designing zoning layouts that balance static pressure while delivering the exact CFM needed to maintain different temperatures throughout your house.

Our Zoning expertize Includes:

  • Zone Controller installation: the "brain" that manages multiple thermostats.
  • Motorized Damper Setup: robotic control over airflow in individual ducts.
  • Bypass duct engineering: managing excess static pressure when only one zone is calling.
  • Smart Thermostat networking: linking multiple sensors and controllers wirelessly.
  • Static pressure Audit: ensuring your system stays within factory blower specs.
