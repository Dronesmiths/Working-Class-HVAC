Stop Fighting Over the Thermostat

Zoning systems use specialized dampers inside your ductwork and multiple thermostats to divide your home into separate comfort areas. This is perfect for two-story homes in the Antelope Valley where the upstairs often remains 10 degrees warmer than the downstairs. Working Class HVAC designs and installs precision zoning systems that eliminate hot and cold spots for good.

Benefits of Zoning:

  • Individualized Temperature: Sleep cool in the master bedroom while keeping the living room at a energy-saving temp.
  • Energy Savings: Reduce airflow to unused areas to lower your utility bills by up to 30%.
  • Extended Equipment Life: Preventing your system from working harder than necessary.
  • Improved Airflow: Directing the exact amount of air needed to problematic rooms.

Is Zoning Right for You?

  • Perfect for homes with "hot spots" in summer.
  • Essential for split-level or two-story properties.
  • Compatible with most modern central HVAC systems.
