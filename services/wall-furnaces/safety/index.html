Expert Safety for High Desert Homes

Because wall furnaces are located directly in your living areas, a cracked heat exchanger or blocked vent isn't just a repair issue—it's a serious health hazard. Working Class HVAC provides rigorous, multi-point safety inspections using electronic carbon monoxide detectors and internal cameras to ensure your unit is safe for operation.

Our Safety Protocol:

  • CO Monitoring: Electronic testing of ambient air while the unit runs.
  • Heat Exchanger Audit: Visual and technical search for hair-line cracks.
  • Venting Inspection: Ensuring the flue is clear of bird nests and debris.
  • Gas Line Verification: Checking every connection for tiny leaks.
  • Control Check: Testing the safety pilot and thermocouple.
