Why Professional Cellar Care?

Standard air conditioners cannot maintain the 55°F temperature and 60-70% humidity required for proper wine aging. Too much humidity leads to mold and label damage; too little causes corks to dry out and oxidize the wine. Working Class HVAC provides specialized refrigeration solutions designed specifically for vapor barriers and thermal envelopes of custom cellars. From WhisperKOOL to Wine Guardian, we service and install the industry's elite platforms.
