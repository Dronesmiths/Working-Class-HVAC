Engineered for Total Vintage Security

A wine cellar is more than a room; it's a controlled thermodynamic environment. If the refrigeration system is undersized or poorly installed, the resulting temperature fluctuations can "cook" your wine in weeks. Working Class HVAC performs comprehensive load calculations based on vapor barrier quality, insulation R-values, and total bottle count to ensure your new system maintains a constant, vibration-free 55°F.

Installation expertise:

  • Split Systems: Putting the noisy compressor outside for silent cellar operation.
  • Ducted Systems: Seamlessly hidden cooling that maintains your cellar's aesthetics.
  • Through-the-Wall units: Efficient, self-contained solutions for smaller collections.
  • Vapor Barrier Integration: Ensuring your HVAC doesn't leak moisture into your walls.
  • High-Precision Controls: Calibration of digital thermostats for ±1°F accuracy.
