Proactive Protection for Your Living Collection

A wine cellar refrigeration unit works significantly harder than a standard AC, maintaining high humidity in a low-temperature environment. This creates unique wear patterns on compressors and evaporator coils. Working Class HVAC provides forensic maintenance that goes beyond simple cleaning—we test vapor barrier integrity, calibrate thermal sensors to 0.1° accuracy, and verify condensate removal to prevent mold growth.

Precision Cellar Checklist:

  • Humidity Sync verification: ensuring your unit isn't stripping too much moisture.
  • Condenser Coil scrubbing: maintaining peak heat rejection for system longevity.
  • Evaporator Bio-Film Audit: preventing organic growth in high-humidity zones.
  • Drain Line Sanitization: preventing clogs that cause water leaks inside the cellar.
  • Refrigerant charge analysis: specialized winter/summer balancing for refrigeration.

The Cellar Priority

  • Constant, unshakeable 55/60 climate stability.
  • Extended lifespan of expensive cellar-grade equipment.
  • Full documentation for insurance of high-value collections.
