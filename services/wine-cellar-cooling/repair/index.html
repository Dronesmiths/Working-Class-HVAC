Rapid Intervention for High-Value Collections

When a wine cellar refrigeration unit fails in the High Desert, the temperature can climb from 55°F to 80°F in just a few hours. This rapid swing can compromise the aging process and damage sensitive labels. Working Class HVAC carries specialized diagnostic tools and parts for cellar-specific units like WhisperKOOL, Breezaire, and CellarPro. We don't just fix the hardware; we stabilize the environment.

Cellar Repair Specialties:

  • Refrigerant Leak Detection: specialized sniffing for high-precision split systems.
  • Evaporator Coil De-Icing: clearing biofilm or ice blockages that kill airflow.
  • Controller Calibration: fixing digital errors in humidity and temp sensors.
  • Vibration Dampening: repairing compressor mounts to protect wine from sediment agitation.
  • Drain Line Clearance: preventing water damage inside your custom wood cellar.
